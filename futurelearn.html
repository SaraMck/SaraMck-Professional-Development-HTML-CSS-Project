Diversity and Inclusion

This course helped me increase my awareness about difference and diversity. I learned about the concept of Pygmalion remodelling â€“ which is a human inclination to change others who are different from you.

I was taught about the difference between ethno-centric and ethno-relative mindsets. An ethno-centric mindset is the belief your own ethnic group is superior to another. An ethno-relative mindset is the ability to understand and accept behaviour under the lens of different cultural contexts.

I discovered more about stereotyping, and of unconscious biases. Unconscious biases are stereotypes that you may have about groups of people that you are not aware that you have. The course explored how to confront these unconscious biases that you may have, and deal with them in a healthy and insightful manner.
